below_eq (int1, int2)

     Usage: Unsigned less than or equal test on int1 and int2.
            Returns TRUE (non-0) if int1 is is less than or
            equal to int2.

Parameters: Int1 and int2 .ints..

   Returns: Non-0 for success, 0 for failure.
